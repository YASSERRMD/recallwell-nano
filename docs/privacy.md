# Privacy Policy

## Fully On-Device

Recallwell Nano runs entirely in your browser. No data leaves your device.

## No Backend Server

There is no backend server. All processing happens locally in your browser.

## No Network Calls at Runtime

After the initial page load, the app makes zero network calls. All data is stored locally in IndexedDB.

## Data Storage

- All documents and chunks are stored in IndexedDB (Dexie)
- No data is sent to external servers
- No analytics or tracking

## AI Processing

- Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano processes queries locally
- No data is sent to Google or any other service
- AI model runs on your device

## Export/Import

- Snapshots are self-contained files
- No external services involved
- You control who has access to your data

## Summary

- Your documents never leave your browser
- Your questions and answers stay on your device
- You have full control over your data
